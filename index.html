ABOUT US

Kent Render is a bespoke render and plaster company that has over 30 years of experience in the commercial sector that now bring premium insulated render systems to the residential market at affordable prices.

We only use premium products and materials from Europe that have self cleaning properties and full European accreditation. Our products come with a 10/20 yr manufacturers warranty. We will save you money on your heating costs and we will put pavement value on the sale of your property.
